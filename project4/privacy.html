Privacy Policy

Last updated July 22, 2026. This policy explains what information strikeworksstudio.com collects, how that information is used, what third parties are involved, and how cookies are handled on this site.

Collection of Information

This site collects information in two ways. First, if you use the contact form, the information you type into the name, email, service, message, and preferred-contact-method fields is sent directly to my email address when you submit the form. I do not store form submissions in any database or public directory; they exist only in my own email inbox once you send them. Second, like most websites, basic technical information may be logged automatically by the hosting provider, such as your browser type, general location, and the pages you visit, for the ordinary purpose of keeping the site running and secure.

Usage of Information

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry, for example to reply about a project quote or a question you asked. I do not use contact-form submissions for marketing, and I do not add anyone to a mailing list without asking first. Basic technical and analytics information, where collected, is used only to understand how the site is performing, such as which pages are visited most and whether pages load quickly, and to fix problems if something breaks.

Third-Party Information Usage

I do not sell, rent, or trade any information collected through this site to third parties. This site may rely on a small number of third-party services to function, such as a web hosting provider and a font or icon delivery service, and those providers may process limited technical data, such as your IP address, purely to deliver their service, for example serving a web font file. Those providers operate under their own privacy policies, and I do not share contact-form content with them.

Cookie Policy

This site does not set its own cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Any cookies present on a page load are limited to those set by third-party resources the page depends on, such as a hosting provider's basic session handling. You can disable or clear cookies at any time through your browser settings; doing so will not affect your ability to read this site's content, though it may occasionally require a font or resource to reload.

Questions

If you have a question about this policy or about information you submitted through the contact form, reach out through the Contact page and I will respond directly.
