Why clients work with me

Fast, hand-coded sites

No page-builder bloat. Every site is written in semantic HTML5 and CSS3 by hand, which keeps pages small and quick to load on a phone on a slow connection.

Accessible by default

Color contrast, alt text, and keyboard navigation are part of the build from day one, not a fix I bolt on after a client complains something does not work.

One person, direct access

You are not routed through an account manager to reach the person actually writing your code. I handle design, development, and support myself.
