How These Images Were Prepared

Each photo was scaled in GIMP to a full version no wider than 600 pixels and a thumbnail no wider than 100 pixels, both maintaining the original aspect ratio, then exported as web-optimized JPEGs so the gallery loads quickly even on a slow connection.
